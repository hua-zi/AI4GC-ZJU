---

AI4GC Lab is a research group at Zhejiang University led by [Shengyu Zhang](/shengyu-zhang). We study efficient AI systems—from multimodal models and computer-use agents to the infrastructure needed to run them reliably outside the lab.

Our work spans edge–cloud collaborative intelligence, agent safety and evaluation, and deployable optimization for real-world settings. We publish at leading venues including CVPR, KDD, and TPAMI, and collaborate with industry partners on production-scale AI systems.
